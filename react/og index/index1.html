What types of legal applications can I create with this tool? +
You can create various types of applications including consumer complaints, small claims, RTI applications, property disputes, and more. The tool guides you through the process based on your specific needs.
How does the voice recognition work with regional languages? +
We use the BHASHINI API which supports multiple Indian languages. Simply select your preferred language and start speaking. The system will recognize and transcribe your speech accurately.
Is my information secure and private? +
Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Your data is encrypted and we don't store any personal information beyond what's necessary for your application. All processing happens securely on our servers.
Do I need internet access to use this tool? +
Yes, an internet connection is required to access our speech recognition, AI processing, and document generation features.
How accurate is the auto-filled application? +
Our system uses advanced AI to accurately extract relevant information from your input. However, we recommend reviewing the auto-filled application before submission to ensure all details are correct.
